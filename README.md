<h1 align="center">🛍️ Zara Sales Analysis Dashboard</h1>
<p align="center"><b>End-to-End Retail Data Analysis using Power BI</b></p>

<hr>

<h2>📌 Overview</h2>
<p>
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Zara’s retail sales data.
It converts raw data into meaningful insights that help in understanding revenue patterns, product performance, and business trends.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Objective</h2>
<p>
The main goal of this project is to analyze sales performance and identify key business drivers.
It focuses on understanding product demand, promotion impact, and store-level optimization opportunities.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🛠️ Tech Stack</h2>
<ul>
  <li><b>Power BI</b> – Dashboard & Visualization</li>
  <li><b>Excel / CSV</b> – Data Source</li>
  <li><b>Power Query</b> – Data Cleaning</li>
  <li><b>DAX</b> – KPI & Measures</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Key Features</h2>

<h3 style="color:#2E86C1;">KPI Summary</h3>
<p>
Total Revenue: <b>33.64M</b> | Total Sales: <b>7996</b> | Avg Price: <b>54.52</b> | Products: <b>5</b>
</p>

<h3 style="color:#2E86C1;">Top Product Analysis</h3>
<p>
Identifies top 10 revenue-generating products, helping understand high-performing items.
</p>

<h3 style="color:#2E86C1;">Category & Section Analysis</h3>
<p>
Analyzes revenue across product categories and sections (Men/Women).
</p>

<h3 style="color:#2E86C1;">Product Position Analysis</h3>
<p>
Compares revenue from End-cap, Aisle, and Front of Store placements.
</p>

<h3 style="color:#2E86C1;">Promotion Impact</h3>
<p>
Compares revenue between promoted and non-promoted products.
</p>

<h3 style="color:#2E86C1;">Seasonality Insights</h3>
<p>
Analyzes seasonal product contribution for better planning.
</p>

<h3 style="color:#2E86C1;">Interactive Filters</h3>
<p>
Dynamic filters for category, section, and seasonal analysis.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>💡 Key Insights</h2>
<ul>
  <li>End-cap placement generates highest revenue</li>
  <li>Majority revenue comes without promotions</li>
  <li>Top products contribute most of the revenue</li>
  <li>Certain categories dominate sales</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📈 Business Value</h2>
<p>
Helps businesses make data-driven decisions by identifying high-performing products and optimizing strategies.
Supports better inventory planning, marketing decisions, and store layout improvements.
</p>

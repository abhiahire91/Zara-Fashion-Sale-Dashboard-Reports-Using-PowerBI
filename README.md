<h2 style="color:#db2777;">📌 Overview</h2>

<div style="background:linear-gradient(135deg,#1f1c2c,#ec4899); padding:12px; border-radius:10px; color:white;">
This project presents an <b>interactive Power BI dashboard</b> designed to analyze Zara’s retail sales data. 
It transforms raw data into meaningful insights to understand <b>revenue trends, product performance, and business patterns</b>.
</div>

<hr>

<h2 style="color:#db2777;">🎯 Objective</h2>

<ul>
<li>Analyze overall sales performance</li>
<li>Identify top-performing products</li>
<li>Understand impact of promotions</li>
<li>Optimize store and product strategies</li>
</ul>
